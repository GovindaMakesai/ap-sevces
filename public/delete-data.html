Data Deletion Instructions

AP Service

If you have an account with AP Service and would like your data associated with our service removed, you may request deletion by email.

How to request deletion

Send an email to aparif786@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account (if applicable). Please include “Data deletion request” in the subject line and briefly describe the account or login method you used (for example, Facebook login).

Processing time

We will process verified deletion requests and remove your associated data within 7 days of confirming your request, unless we are required to retain certain information by law.

Questions

For other privacy questions, see our Privacy Policy or contact aparif786@gmail.com.
